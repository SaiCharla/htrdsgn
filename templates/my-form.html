This program generates all possible heaters connections for given power and length in ft.


Usage:
Enter the required Wattage and length separated by space and click submit.

eg: 35 1
For a 1 ft 35 watt heater


Note:
Length of the heater should be provided in ft.
Default factor of safety is 1.5
Default tolerance is 0.5 ft


For changing factor of safety and tolerence use -f and -tol flags.

eg: 35 1 -f 2 -tol 0.25
For factor of safety 2 and tolerence 0.25ft
